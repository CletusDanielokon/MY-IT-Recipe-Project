Mexican Carne Asada

Savor the ultimate Mexican street-food classic at home. Our authentic Carne Asada features tender flank steak marinated in a vibrant blend of fresh citrus juices, garlic, jalapeño, and smoky cumin. Grilled over high heat to achieve a perfect, charred crust while remaining juicy on the inside, it is ideal for slicing thin to stuff into warm corn tortillas, top loaded nachos, or fill epic burritos.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl or zip-top bag, whisk together the lime juice, orange juice, olive oil, minced garlic, chopped jalapeño, cilantro, cumin, salt, and pepper. Add the steak, ensuring it is completely coated. Seal and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or up to 8 hours for maximum flavor.

step 2

Preheat your outdoor grill or indoor cast-iron grill pan to high heat. Remove the steak from the marinade, wiping away excess herbs so they don't burn, and discard the remaining liquid. Sear the steak for about 5 to 7 minutes per side, depending on thickness, until beautifully charred.

step 3

Transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for 10 minutes to lock in the juices. Slice the meat against the grain into thin strips, then chop into bite-sized pieces. Serve immediately with warm tortillas, fresh lime wedges, and salsa.

ingredients

2 lbs flank steak (or skirt steak)

1/3 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped

1/2 cup olive oil

Juice of 2 fresh limes

Juice of 1 fresh orange

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced

1 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp black pepper

tools

Outdoor Grill or Cast-Iron Grill Pan

Large Chef's Knife & Cutting Board

Tongs (for flipping the steak)

Whisk & Mixing Bowl

Measuring Cups & Spoons
