Yogurt Fruit Parfait

Brighten up your morning with this vibrant, refreshing Greek yogurt fruit parfait. This crowd-pleasing breakfast or healthy dessert features alternating layers of rich, creamy vanilla Greek yogurt, a colorful medley of fresh sweet berries, and crunchy honey-oat granola. Drizzled with a touch of pure honey or maple syrup, it strikes the ultimate balance between smooth, crisp, tangy, and sweet textures. It is as beautiful to look at in a clear glass as it is delicious to eat.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

10 servings

Tags : strawberry breakfast fried festive

instructions

step 1

Wash your fresh strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries thoroughly under cold water. Pat them completely dry with a paper towel so they do not water down the yogurt. Slice the strawberries into bite-sized pieces and toss all the berries together gently in a mixing bowl.

step 2

Take a clean, clear glass or mason jar to show off the visual layers. Spoon 2 to 3 large tablespoons of Greek yogurt into the very bottom of the glass to create an even first layer. Follow this with a generous handful of the mixed berry medley, spreading it all the way to the edges of the glass.

step 3

Sprinkle a layer of crunchy granola directly over the berries, then drizzle a teaspoon of honey or maple syrup across the top. Repeat the layers—yogurt, berries, and granola—one more time until the glass is filled to the top. Finish with a final beautiful berry garnish and a fresh mint leaf, serving immediately while the granola stays perfectly crisp.

ingredients

2 cups plain or vanilla Greek Yogurt

1/2 cup fresh Strawberries, sliced

1/2 cup fresh Blueberries

1/2 cup fresh Raspberries

1 cup crunchy Honey-Oat Granola

2 tablespoons organic Honey or Maple Syrup

Fresh Mint leaves (for garnish)

tools

Clear Glassware, Goblets, or Mason Jars

Small Paring Knife & Cutting Board

Mixing Bowl & Spoons

Measuring Cups & Spoons

Paper Towels (for drying fruit)
