Classic Nigerian Fried Rice

A stellar highlight of any festive spread, Nigerian Fried Rice is a vibrant, savory dish known for its signature golden-green color and crisp vegetable medley. Long-grain parboiled rice is rich-infused by simmering it directly in a deeply seasoned beef or chicken stock. It is then flash-fried in small batches with liver chunks, sweet corn, carrots, green peas, and aromatic spices like curry and thyme. Light, non-greasy, and bursting with color, it pairs perfectly with fried plantains and roasted chicken.

prep time

50 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : rice breakfast fried festive

instructions

step 1

Thoroughly wash your rice to remove excess starch, parboil it for 10 minutes, and drain. In a pot, bring your seasoned meat stock to a boil along with 1 tablespoon of curry powder. Add the parboiled rice to the boiling stock, cover tightly, and cook on medium heat for 15 to 18 minutes until the liquid is fully absorbed and the grains are firm but cooked through. Spread the rice out on a tray to cool down completely.

step 2

While the rice cools, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large wok or deep frying pan over medium-high heat. Sauté your chopped onions and minced garlic for 1 minute until fragrant. Toss in the diced carrots, green beans, liver pieces, and seasoning cube, stir-frying for 3 to 4 minutes until the vegetables start to soften but still retain a crisp bite. Fold in the sweet corn and green peas for the final 60 seconds.

step 3

Work in small batches to keep the heat high: add a few scoops of the cooked, cooled rice into the frying pan with a portion of the vegetable-meat stir-fry. Toss and fry vigorously for 2 to 3 minutes so the flavors coat every grain uniformly. Transfer the batch to a clean insulated server and repeat the process for the remaining rice and vegetables. Serve hot!

ingredients

3 cups Long-Grain Parboiled Rice

3 cups rich Beef or Chicken Stock

1/2 cup Cow Liver (boiled and finely diced)

1 cup Carrots, finely diced

1/2 cup Green Beans or Runner Beans, chopped

1/2 cup Sweet Corn (canned)

1/2 cup Green Peas

1 medium Red Onion, finely chopped

2 tbsp Jamaican Curry Powder & 1 tsp Thyme

3 tbsp Vegetable Oil (divided for batch frying)

2 chicken Seasoning Cubes & salt to taste

tools

Large Wok or Deep Non-Stick Frying Pan

Medium Boiling Pot with tight-fitting lid

Large Cooking Tray (for cooling the rice)

Sharp Chef's Knife & Cutting Board

Measuring Cups & Spoons
