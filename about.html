Simple Recipes

Simple Recipes is an interactive, mobile-responsive recipe directory and food blog built to showcase delicious, everyday meals—with a special spotlight on authentic Nigerian dishes like Smoky Jollof Rice, Edikang Ikong/Efo Riro (Vegetable Soup), and Boiled Yam with Egg Sauce.

At its core, the website is about culinary simplicity and accessibility. It strips away the clutter found on traditional cooking sites to give food lovers exactly what they need: beautiful food photography, minimal reading, organized recipe tags, and instant access to critical cooking metrics like prep times, cook times, and serving sizes. Whether a user is browsing on a phone in the kitchen or a desktop at home, the platform provides a clean, stress-free layout for discovering their next meal.
