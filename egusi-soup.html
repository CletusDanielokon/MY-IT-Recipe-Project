Nigerian Egusi Soup (Melon Seed Soup)

A beloved West African classic, Egusi soup is a rich, savory dish thickened with ground melon seeds and simmered in a vibrant palm oil broth. Loaded with tender beef, smoked fish, and nutrient-rich leafy greens, this deeply aromatic soup delivers a perfect balance of earthy, nutty flavors and gentle heat. It is traditionally served alongside swallows like pounded yam, eba, or fufu for the ultimate comfort meal.

prep time

40 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Soup swallow nigerian dinner

instructions

step 1

Blend your melon seeds with a little water or stock until a thick, smooth paste forms, then set it aside. In your pot, heat the palm oil on medium heat for 2 minutes (do not bleach it). Add your chopped onions and locust beans (iru), and sauté them for about 3 minutes until fragrant.

step 2

Scoop small lumps of the egusi paste into the hot oil, or stir it in smoothly depending on your preference. Cover and let it fry on low-medium heat for about 10 minutes, stirring frequently so it doesn't burn, until the egusi forms firm, distinct curd-like textures.

step 3

Slowly pour in your rich meat stock and add the cooked beef, shaki, and smoked fish. Stir gently, cover, and let it simmer for 15 minutes to marry the flavors. Finally, stir in your ground crayfish, seasoned pepper mix, and shredded ugwu leaves. Simmer for 3 more minutes until the greens soften, then remove from heat.

ingredients

2 cups ground Egusi (melon seeds)

1/2 cup pure Red Palm Oil

1 lb mixed meats (cooked Beef, Shaki, or Goat meat)

1 medium Smoked Fish (cleaned and deboned)

2 cups rich Meat Stock

1 cup fresh Ugwu (fluted pumpkin leaves) or Spinach, shredded

3 tbsp ground Crayfish

2 tbsp Iru (fermented locust beans)

Blended pepper mix (1 Scotch bonnet, 2 bell peppers, 1 onion)

2 seasoning cubes & salt to taste

tools

High-Speed Blender (for egusi and peppers)

Large Cooking Pot with Lid

Wooden Stirring Spoon (Oko-iyanu)

Sharp Chef's Knife & Cutting Board

Measuring Cups & Spoons
