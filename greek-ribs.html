Greek Lemon Garlic Ribs

Bring the bright, bold flavors of the Mediterranean to your table with these crispy Greek ribs. Unlike sticky BBQ styles, these ribs are baked or grilled until tender, then tossed in a vibrant, zesty dressing of extra virgin olive oil, freshly squeezed lemon juice, minced garlic, and wild dried oregano. The result is a perfect balance of savory, tangy, and herby notes with a beautifully rendered, crispy outer crust.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Greek keto ribs dinner

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Pat the ribs dry with a paper towel and remove the thin membrane from the back of the bone rack. Season both sides generously with sea salt, coarse black pepper, and 1 tablespoon of dried oregano, pressing the seasoning firmly into the meat.

step 2

Place the ribs in a deep baking dish or roasting pan, bone-side down. Pour 1/2 cup of water or chicken broth into the bottom of the pan (avoiding pouring it over the seasoned meat). Cover the pan tightly with heavy-duty aluminum foil to trap the steam, and bake for 1.5 to 2 hours until the meat is completely tender.

step 3

While the ribs rest, whisk together the fresh lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, and the remaining oregano in a bowl. Uncover the ribs, brush them generously with half of the lemon-garlic mixture, and broil or grill on high heat for 3 to 5 minutes until the edges turn golden and crispy. Slice into individual ribs, toss with the remaining dressing, and serve hot.

ingredients

3 lbs Baby Back Ribs (or St. Louis style ribs)

1/3 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 large lemons)

1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

6 cloves garlic, finely minced

2 tbsp dried Greek oregano

1 tsp sea salt

1/2 tsp coarsely cracked black pepper

1/2 cup chicken broth or water (for steaming)

tools

Deep Roasting Pan & Aluminum Foil

Oven Broiler or Outdoor Grill

Small Whisk & Mixing Bowl

Pastry Brush (for basting)

Sharp Chef's Knife & Cutting Board
